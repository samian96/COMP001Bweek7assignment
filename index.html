Questions about COMP001B

What worked well over the last eight weeks and aided your learning process?

One thing that helped a lot with learning our css concepts for me where the linked in videos when we had access to them. They really broke down the concepts step by step to help better understand when and where to use something.

What would you change about the course?

The only thing I would change is the amount of time, but even with the fast pace of this class I feel I grasped and remembered a lot of the concepts that we learned.

Did you learn what you expected to learn in this course?

I learned exactly what I was expecting in this class, which was the different ways to styling a webpage using css. For me this has been one of the more fun classes in this major so I quiet enjoyed these last 8 weeks.

What concepts were new to you, and what concepts have you already encountered in the past?

One of the new concepts I encountered was the use of the grid system, for me I personally enjoyed using the float function more but I understand why the grid system is much stronger in styling. Older concepts I remember from html/css class would be the use of parent selectors and child selectors, which are very useful. Typography was also a new concept I had not previously known about, but I enjoyed learning it.
